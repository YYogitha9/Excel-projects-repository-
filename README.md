# 🚴‍♂️ Bike Sales Data Analysis (Excel Project)

## 📌 Overview
This Excel project was completed during a **Data Technician Bootcamp** and focuses on analysing a **bike sales dataset** to uncover trends in customer demographics, purchasing behaviour, and regional performance.

The project demonstrates practical use of Excel for **data cleaning, analysis, and visualisation**, transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights.

---

## 🛠️ Key Skills Demonstrated

### 🔢 Excel Formulas & Functions
Used a variety of Excel functions to perform calculations and enhance data analysis:
- `SUM` / `SUMIF` – calculate total and conditional sales values  
- `AVERAGE` / `AVERAGEIF` – analyse average sales performance  
- `DATE`, `MONTH`, `YEAR` – extract and manipulate date information  
- `UNIQUE` – identify distinct customer groups and categories  
- `VLOOKUP` – retrieve related data across multiple tables  
- `SWITCH` – classify sales into categories (e.g. Low, Medium, High)  

---

### 📂 Data Cleaning & Organisation
- Applied **filtering** to focus on specific customer groups and regions  
- Used **sorting** to organise sales records and identify patterns  
- Structured datasets for easier analysis and reporting  

---

### 📊 Data Analysis
- Built **PivotTables** to summarise bike sales data by:
  - Region / Country  
  - Age group  
  - Gender  
  - Product category  
- Identified key insights such as:
  - Highest performing regions  
  - Customer demographics driving sales  
  - Trends in purchasing behaviour  

---

### 📈 Data Visualisation
- Created charts to visually represent sales trends and comparisons  
- Used Excel dashboards to improve **data storytelling**
- Highlighted insights such as top-performing segments and sales distribution  

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Bike Sales dataset (customer demographics, purchase behaviour, and sales performance)
